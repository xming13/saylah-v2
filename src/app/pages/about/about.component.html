About SayLah!
Welcome to SayLah! - a Singapore-based free program dedicated to empowering non-verbal individuals to find their voice. At SayLah!, we understand the challenges faced by non-verbal individuals in expressing themselves and communicating with others. Our mission is to bridge this gap by providing accessible and user-friendly tools and resources that facilitate communication and self-expression. Through innovative technologies and compassionate support, SayLah! strives to make communication more inclusive and equitable for everyone. We believe that every individual deserves to be heard and understood, regardless of their verbal abilities. SayLah! is not just a program; it's a community of caregivers, educators, therapists, and individuals committed to advocating for the rights and dignity of non-verbal individuals. Together, we're breaking down barriers and empowering individuals to express themselves authentically and confidently. Join us on our journey to give a voice to those who have been silenced. Together, let's SayLah! - because everyone deserves to be heard.
